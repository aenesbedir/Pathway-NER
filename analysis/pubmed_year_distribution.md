## Notes

- Skew toward 2000–2010 in all articles is expected: KEGG and Reactome cite original landmark discovery papers, most of which predate 2010.
- Full-text articles skew newer (54% from ≥2010) because Open Access publishing became widespread after 2008.
- Older text is still valid for NER training.
- Option for future improvement: supplement with a date-filtered PubMed search (≥2015) to increase modern-language coverage.
